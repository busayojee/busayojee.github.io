ONE DAY

By Mayowa on June 15

Most motivational speakers, relationship counselors and life coaches tell you about “one day”. “one day, life will have meaning” “One day, you’ll find the one who loves you and who’ll stick with you”. “One day you’ll drive the car you want”. And they all say it like one day is a specific day on the calendar that we all know.

Because of one day, people have stopped appreciating today. I get that most of us are not where we want to be and all, but can we all agree that we’re not where we were last year or some months ago. You might have just been promoted to the next class,or even done something “as little” as finishing a book, you might have learnt a skill, worked on your anger, etc. Why don’t we stop and acknowledge the efforts we’re putting because most of us don’t know when our one day would come and we’ve stopped living our lives for us, we only live it for one day. And in the end, one day comes and becomes today and the journey for another “one day” begins
